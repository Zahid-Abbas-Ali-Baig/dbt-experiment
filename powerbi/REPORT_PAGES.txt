# ShopSphere Power BI report — build visuals per page

Open `shopsphere.pbix`, then run `python powerbi/setup_all.py` (model measures only).
Optionally run `python powerbi/inject_report.py powerbi/shopsphere.pbix` for empty named pages.

Use measures from `_KPI Measures` (not raw column sums).

## Page 1 — Executive Dashboard
- Cards: Net Revenue, Order Count, Avg Order Value, Customer Count, Refund Rate %
- Line chart: Net Revenue by order_month
- Column chart: Order Count by month
- Donut: Net Revenue by marketing_channel_name

## Page 2 — Geographic Performance (maps)
- Filled map: Location=country, Values=Net Revenue
- Filled map: Location=country, Values=Customer Count
- Bubble map: Location=city + country, Size=Net Revenue
- Bar chart: country x Net Revenue + Customer Count

## Page 3 — Customer Intelligence
- Bar Top 10: name x Customer Lifetime Net Spend
- Donut: customer_segment x Net Revenue
- Treemap: lifetime_value_segment x Customer Lifetime Net Spend
- Scatter: lifetime_order_count vs lifetime_net_spend

## Page 4 — Products and Categories
- Bar: category_name x Category Net Revenue
- Bar: category_name x Units Sold
- Table: product_name, category_name, Category Net Revenue, Units Sold

## Page 5 — Marketing and Acquisition
- Bar: marketing_channel_name x Net Revenue
- Bar: marketing_channel_name x Avg Order Value
- 100% stacked column: month x channel x Order Count

## Page 6 — Revenue and Refunds
- Clustered bar: month x Gross Revenue, Total Refunds, Net Revenue
- Line: Gross Revenue vs Net Revenue by month
- Donut: order_status x Net Revenue

## Page 7 — Order Operations
- Donut: order_status x Order Count
- Cards: Refund Rate %, Units per Order
- Line: Refund Rate % by month

## Page 8 — Semantic Model Health
- KPI cards + table: country, city, Net Revenue, Customer Count
